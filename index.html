Welcome to the Pikachu Homepage

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon known for its lightning bolt-shaped tail and its ability to generate electricity. It is one of the most recognizable and beloved Pokémon in the world.

Some interesting facts about Pikachu:

  • Pikachu evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship and evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone.
  • It is the mascot of the Pokémon franchise and often appears alongside its trainer, Ash Ketchum, in the Pokémon animated series.
  • Pikachu's cry sounds like "Pika Pika!"
